# Boardwalk Games

## Goals for the Website

### Business Goals

The primary goal is to **increase in-store foot traffic and customer engagement**.  In support of this:

* Showcase the shop's services
* Increase brand awareness
* Provide essential information
* Encourage group visits and event participation

### User Personas

Boardwalk Games will appeal to people who enjoy socialising and playing board games.

Specifically:

* Casual gamers and families looking for a fun, relaxed atmosphere
* Enthusiasts seeking information about events and new releases
* Students looking for budget-friendly options and discounts
